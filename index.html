Programming Portfolio

Adobe Skills

Adobe After Effects

I first used adobe after effects in 2012. My uncle works for adobe and was able to get me a copy for free. I learned the ropes on my own from the knowledge I had from using window movie maker. I used after effects for years unknowing of Premiere and its wonderful abilities.

Adobe Photoshop

I began using photoshop in 2014 to create funny photos for my friends and I to laugh at. It quickly became daily, that I was using Photoshop to create thumbnails and graphics for my YouTube Channel. I would help my friends create their thumbnails too, so I have some teaching experience.

Adobe Premier Pro

I began editing YouTube videos with Adobe Premiere in 2016, which was pretty late into my YouTube career and would've saved me a lot of time and effort I put in using After Effects. It changed my whole world and cut down 4 hour editing periods into 1 hour speed edits.

Adobe Illustrator

I had always wondered why It was so hard to create shapes and paths in photoshop, but then I learned all about Illustrator in my first year college graphic design class. Now I am a wizard with the pen tool and love creating original artworks from scratch such this page above the fold.
